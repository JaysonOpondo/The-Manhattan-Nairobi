About Us

The Manhattan Nairobi is more than just a restaurant and bar; it's an experience. Nestled in the heart of Nairobi, we offer a sophisticated ambiance, exquisite culinary delights, and a curated selection of beverages. Our commitment is to provide an unforgettable experience for every guest, whether you're here for a casual drink, a romantic dinner, or a lively event.

Our chefs craft dishes with the freshest local ingredients, blending international flavors with a touch of Kenyan heritage. Our mixologists are artists, creating classic cocktails and innovative new drinks that tantalize the senses. Come and discover why The Manhattan Nairobi is the city's premier destination for fine dining and entertainment.
